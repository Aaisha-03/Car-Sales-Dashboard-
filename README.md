This project is an interactive Power BI  dashboard that shows car sales performance in Norway for different automobile brands. 
The dashboard brings multiple KPIs into one view so users can quickly understand which brands and models are performing best in the market.
Brand Market Value – Column chart comparing market value (in millions) for major brands such as Volkswagen, BMW, Audi, Ford, Skoda, Toyota, and Hyundai.
Top 5 Models by Revenue – Bar chart showing the highest-earning car models with their revenue contribution.
Total Units Sold & Variants – KPI cards summarizing total units sold (in thousands) and total number of car variants available.
Total Revenue – KPI card highlighting the overall revenue generated in the market.
Sales Trend Over Time – Line chart visualizing how sales have changed over different years.
Market Share – Pie chart showing each brand’s share in the Norwegian car market.
Brand Logos & Image – Brand logos and a featured car image to make the dashboard more engaging and visually appealing.
